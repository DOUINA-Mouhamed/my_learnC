For this first exercice you have to calculate the circumference of a circle :

* The value of PI must be 3.14159
* You have to ask user for the radius of their circle
* You are not allowed to use internet
* You can add the calculation of the circle's area as a bonus

Example :

[douina@fedora mini_projets]$ ./cercle 
Enter the radius of your circle : 10

The circumference is : 31.42
[douina@fedora mini_projets]$

Exercice level : easy
Hint : PI * D
